Some insights of the school

  • Haileybury is an English co-educational public school located in Hertford Heath, Hertfordshire
  • It is a member of the Rugby Group and enrols pupils at the 11+, 13+ and 16+ stages of education
  • Over 890 pupils attend Haileybury, of whom more than 550 board
  • The campus occupies over 500 acres of Hertfordshire countryside, approximately 20 miles from London
